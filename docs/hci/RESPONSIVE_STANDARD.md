# Responsive standard

## Viewports to verify

320×568, 360×800, 390×844, 412×915, 768×1024, 1024×768, 1280×720, 1366×768, 1440×900, 1920×1080

## Rules

- No horizontal overflow on core flows  
- Touch targets ≥ 44×44  
- Sticky bars must not hide primary submit  
- Tables: sticky header + horizontal scroll affordance; critical ops lists may use cards on small screens  
- Preview mobile frame: `width: min(100%, 390px)`  
- Forms usable at 320px width
